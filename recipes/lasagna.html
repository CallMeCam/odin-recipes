Lasagna

Recipe Description

Lasagna is a fantastic and easy dish to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Sitr in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 90 minutes.
